In this assignment, we've learned about the three most popular Frameworks used to speed up styling.
We learned about Bootstrap, Pico, and Tailwind. In the beginning, I've found it tedious and couldn't see its purpose, but while working on the assignment I saw some uses I could advantage of in the future, like fast preset stylings for nav bars, forms, buttons and UI Cards. I chose to use Pico just because I found Bootstrap messed too much on the HTML file, and I liked that Pico works just with HTML semantics.
Still, I think it has a "template feel" around that doesn't convince me 100%.
